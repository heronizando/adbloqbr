Frequently Asked Questions

What is AdBloq BR?

AdBloq BR is an open-source ad blocker designed to enhance your browsing experience by blocking unwanted ads and protecting your online privacy. It is specially optimized for the Brazilian market.

How does AdBloq BR differ from other solutions?

Unlike many traditional tools, AdBloq BR is an open-source project focused on the Brazilian market, allowing for community-driven improvements and transparency in its ad-blocking methods.

Is AdBloq BR free to use?

Yes, AdBloq BR is completely free. Being an open-source ad blocker, it is developed and maintained by a community of volunteers dedicated to improving the online experience.

How do I install AdBloq BR?

You can install AdBloq BR by visiting our download page and following the instructions for your specific browser. It's compatible with most major web browsers.

Can AdBloq BR block all types of ads?

AdBloq BR is effective at blocking a wide range of ads, including pop-ups, banners, and video ads. However, some highly integrated or native ads might not be consistently blocked.

Does AdBloq BR also block trackers?

Yes, apart from being an ad blocker, AdBloq BR also offers protection against various online trackers, helping to maintain your privacy while browsing.

Will using AdBloq BR slow down my browser?

No, in fact, using AdBloq BR typically results in faster browsing speeds as it prevents resource-heavy ads from loading.

Can I customize the ad-blocking features of AdBloq BR?

Yes, AdBloq BR offers customizable settings, allowing you to tailor the ad-blocking experience to your preferences, including whitelisting your favorite websites.

Is it legal to use an ad blocker like AdBloq BR?

Yes, using an ad blocker is legal. However, some websites may request you to disable ad blockers to access their content.

How can I contribute to the AdBloq BR project?

We welcome contributions! You can contribute by coding, reporting bugs, suggesting features, or helping with documentation. Check out our GitHub repository for more details.
