Introduction

At AdBloq BR, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and sharing of information when using our website or extension.

1. Information Collection

a. Automatically Collected Data

We may collect non-personal data automatically when you use our website or extension, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address and approximate location (country, city)
  • URLs visited
  • Date and time of visits
  • Device type (desktop or mobile)
  • Website usage statistics

b. Personal Information

If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information such as:

  • Your name and email address
  • Contents of your message

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, improve, and maintain our services
  • Understand user behavior and interactions
  • Communicate updates and support
  • Conduct analytics and generate anonymized insights

3. Information Sharing

  • We do not sell or transfer your personal information to third parties
  • We may share anonymized data for analytical purposes
  • We may disclose data to comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • Data is stored on secured servers
  • Access is restricted to authorized personnel

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict data processing

6. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
